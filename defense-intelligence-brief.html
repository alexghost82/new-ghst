The Gap // Footage Is Not Intelligence
02 / 06
Why isn't the footage we already record enough?

The footage already exists. The understanding doesn't.

Control rooms are saturated with feeds no one can watch in full. Critical moments are buried in hours of recording, scattered across dozens of cameras, recoverable only after the fact.

Ghost closes that gap — every camera becomes a conversation, every group of cameras becomes an investigation channel, and every recorded moment becomes textual memory you can search by simply asking.

  • Plain-language investigation
    Reconstruct an event across multiple cameras by asking, not searching.
  • Shift & sector summaries
    Generate handover and situation summaries for any timeframe or group of cameras.
  • Cross-camera channels
    Group cameras like conversations and investigate a whole sector as one thread.

Doctrine // Three Operational Layers
03 / 06
How does Ghost actually work for an operator?

Ghost operates across three layers — talk to the past, the present, and a future you define yourself.

  • History you can talk toMemory
    Every sequence is continuously converted into rich, searchable text. Ask what happened at a location and timeframe and get an answer in seconds — no scrubbing, no jumping between cameras.
  • Ask what is happening nowLive
    Query any camera or sector in real time and receive a plain-language description of the current situation, on demand.
  • Checks you define in natural languageWatch
    Describe a recurring check in your own words. Ghost runs exactly what you defined and flags deviations — it executes your intent, it does not decide on its own.
“Watch the loading bay and alert me the moment the emergency exit is blocked.”

Data Flow // Agentless By Design
04 / 06
How does Ghost connect to our cameras?

Four stages. One direction. No agents on your cameras. Ghost ingests streams from your existing infrastructure over RTSP, isolated HDMI capture, or local integrations. Frames are interpreted into structured narratives and context, then surfaced to operators as plain-language conversations — without exposing raw footage beyond your boundary.

Compliance // Security Framework
06 / 06
How do you handle security, retention, and assurance?

Ghost is built around a zero-trust security model. Raw video exposure is minimized; structured memory layers persist instead, and all communication moves over encrypted channels end to end.

  • Zero-trust architecture
    Every request authenticated; no implicit trust between components.
  • Access control & audit logs
    Role-based access with configurable, exportable audit trails and chain of custody.
  • Encrypted channels
    All communication moves over encrypted channels, end to end.
  • Data minimization
    Raw video exposure is minimized; structured memory layers persist instead.
